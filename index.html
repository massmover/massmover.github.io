Commander MultiBot

Move All Users From a Discord Channel Fast !

How To Use:

  1. Invite the Commander Bot to your discord server.
  2. If you are moving up to 10 users, add the LVL 1 Servant
  3. If you are moving more than 10 users, add the LVL 1 and LVL 2 Servants
  4. If you are moving more than 20 users, add the LVL 1, LVL 2 and LVL 3 Servants
  5. Make sure the channels are not blocking "Move" permissions.
  6. Type "-c help" in a channel the bot can read and write.

    7. Done!
